1. Overview and objectives
    Diabetes is a growing public health challenge across Africa, with women being disproportionately affected due to:
- Limited access to early diagnosis
- High maternal risks related to gestational diabetes
- Late or incorrect diagnoses leading to complications

    This project seeks to bridge the healthcare gap by using AI to detect and distinguish between types of diabetes, such as:
- Type 2 Diabetes
- Gestational Diabetes

    By improving diagnostic accuracy, we enable earlier intervention, better health outcomes, and ultimately, saved lives.
- Gender- and race-aware model fairness checks
- Transparent predictions (explainable AI)
- Avoiding biases or misdiagnosis consequences
